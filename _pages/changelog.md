### `Initial Release`
# **Version 1.0**
Welcome to **Daily Glow** — your personalized beauty, wellness, and aesthetic transformation app. This first release lays the foundation for building consistent, sustainable habits that help you become your most radiant self.

#### What's New
- Personalized onboarding experience to understand your goals, lifestyle, and dream aesthetic  
- Dream Aesthetic selection including Clean Girl, Cottagecore, E-Girl, Summer Glow, Surprise Me, and Custom  
- Goal-based planning for glowing skin, body sculpting, hair growth, and full transformations  
- AI-powered facial analysis with insights on skin quality, symmetry, face shape, undertones, and best features  
- Overall Aesthetic Potential score with percentile comparisons  
- Daily Glow tasks across skincare, hair care, fitness & movement, makeup/style, and self-care  
- Streak tracking, longest streaks, badges, and daily completion circles  
- Progress tracking with activity charts, recent scans, and progress photos  
- Before & after transformation previews to visualize your glow journey  
- Smart notifications to support consistency without overwhelm  
- Secure sign-in with Apple and Google to save your progress
